I know I should have talked to you before ending things like that. I know I should have been more understanding. I know you were busy, and I understand that you have a household to manage and that your focus right now is your career. I’m so sorry. I promise I’ll never hurt you again. I promise to always communicate before making any stupid decisions. Please give me one more chance. I miss you so much. I miss how we used to be. I miss your smile, your jokes, and the way you used to annoy me. I love you so much, more than you can ever imagine. I want to grow old with you. I want to see you win, buy that BMW you have always dreamed of, and watch you achieve it with happy tears in my eyes. I want to see you take them out, send them to school, and even get them married and I want us to see it all together. I know I have been very cold towards you, and every time we fight, I realize how much you mean to me and how much I love you. I hope we never fall out of love with each other and that we always win. Please give me a chance to make this right. I love you, always and forever.❤️
